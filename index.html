About Me

I am a highly motivated and results-driven Ph.D. student specializing in Analog Mixed-Signal Design at the Indian Institute of Science Education and Research (IISER) Bhopal. With a strong foundation in Analog Design & Layout, I have hands-on experience in Circuit Design, Simulation, and Post Layout Simulations.
